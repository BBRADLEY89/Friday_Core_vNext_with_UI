SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT

This Software Development Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2025, between TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Client"), and DevStudio LLC, a California limited liability company ("Developer").

1. SCOPE OF WORK
Developer shall design, develop, and deliver a custom web application for Client's e-commerce platform according to the specifications outlined in Exhibit A.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Developer a total fee of $50,000 for the services outlined in this Agreement. Payment shall be made as follows:
- 25% ($12,500) due upon signing this Agreement
- 50% ($25,000) due upon delivery of beta version  
- 25% ($12,500) due upon final delivery and acceptance

Payment is due within 30 days of invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

3. DELIVERY SCHEDULE
Developer shall deliver the beta version within 60 days of contract execution.
Final delivery shall occur within 90 days of contract execution.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All source code, documentation, and deliverables created under this Agreement shall be the exclusive property of Client upon full payment. Developer retains rights to general methodologies and know-how.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information shared during the course of this Agreement for a period of 5 years.

6. LIABILITY AND WARRANTIES
Developer warrants that the software will be free from material defects for 90 days after delivery. Developer's total liability shall not exceed the contract value of $50,000.

7. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for all work completed to date.

8. PAYMENT DEADLINE OVERRIDE
Notwithstanding Section 2, Client may request immediate payment upon delivery of any milestone, with payment due within 5 business days of such request.

9. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
